How Crixus fits into the picture?

With more to do and but less people to do it, companies today face the difficult decision of where best to focus their attention in order to make the biggest impact. A well assigned predictive maintenance resource can bring enormous benefits to a company’s bottom line, proactively identifying a potential system failure and get ahead of the problem before it starts.

Industries We Serve

Automotive

Few industries are as challenging as the automotive market. Strict quality standards, high volumes, and tight production deadlines mean that there is little to no room for errors in the manufacturing process.

General Industrial

There is a prevalent need for water and lubricant filtration in a variety of industrial manufacturing application.

Pulp & Paper

Pall is a large global supplier of filtration solutions designed for pulp and paper applications. Pall maintains close ties with pulp and paper industrial manufacturing leaders worldwide.

Mining

The mining business relies on heavy equipment and a consistent supply of water to maintain operations.

Primary Metals

Power Generation
